Penneyville Library

The Pennyville Children's Library does not have permanet staff, it relies on staff from other libraries to come on selected hours of the week to run it. The library is a container structed library and small in size but does not refrain it from providing good service to the children, young adults and community as a whole of Pennyville It holds story times twice a week, on Mondays and Wednesdays. The library has picture books, books that are easy to read and also reference materials for kids. It is situated in same yard as PennyVille creche and most of the kids coming from the creche utilizes it.
