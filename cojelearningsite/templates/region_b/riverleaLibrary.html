Riverlea Library

Riverlea Library provides a quiet area for study purposes and collaboration. The facility provides access to electronic resources and actively participate in the Scuffle Quiz competition, Book reading Skirmish competition and the Battle of the Books Reading competition. The schools involved are Riverlea Primary School, Al-Azar Institute and Wilhelmina Hoskins Primary School. The programs target Grade 4 and Grade 7 learners respectively. The library is also responsible for Early Childhood Development (ECD) Programmes. The Program caters for Fourteen (14) Creches and Nursery Schools in the region The library also conducts outreach and holiday programmes for the community.
