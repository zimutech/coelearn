Parkview Library

Parkview Library is in partnership with Pam Goldings by cleaning the library garden once a weekly and also working with the Residence Association during Parkview literary festival. Wi-Fi is available free of charge in the library. July and December school holidays programmes are planned regularly and children are read stories. These are always well attended and enjoyed by the children and their parents/caregivers. Other programs include storytelling sessions for 0-3 years and 3-8 years every Monday, people with disabilities once a quarter, book clubs every month for adults and Vuleka primary school visits the library every Friday for storytelling through music.
