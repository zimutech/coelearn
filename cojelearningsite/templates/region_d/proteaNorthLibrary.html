Protea North Library

The Protea North Library is staffed by a senior Librarian, two Library Assistants, General Assistant and a relief worker. The collection at Protea North Library costists of adult and junior fiction and non-fiction books as well as a collection of reference books. The library consists of two floors. The upper level is used for study purposes mainly for tertiary students. WI-FI is availabe free of charge in the library. During the school holiday the library programmes for children and youth. The library has a story times every Thursday morning for pre-school. The library has programmes for primary school learners, named Story Skirmish and Battle of the Books. There is also Science Scuffle programmes aimed at equipting primary school learners with the skills to do information searches using reference material. The library has a reading club for children named Bala Le Rona. We have a vibrant soulbuddys club that meet weekly in the library.
