Halfway House Library

Halfway House Library is committed to helping children and adults in order to develop their skills so that both the youth and adults thrive in the global information society. The Library is situated in a safer business park with many companies surrounding it. It is close to Mall of Africa and walking distance to Boulders Mall. Provision of free and safe library access to deliver lending services, educational support, reference and research services and study space. Provision of free and guided access knowledge and information to assist users of all ages in their need for information, education, recreation and lifelong learning, reading promotion such as story telling, holiday programs, displays and reading programs to encourage reading and learning. Programme implementation: eLearning i.e. digital transformation services, eWorld, techno literacy skills, education and information literacy, reading development programmes, outreach services toECD centres, schools and home based centres.
