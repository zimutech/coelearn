Klipfontein View Library

Klipfontein View Library is situated in the Klipfontein Multipurpose Centre. The library has partnerships with creches, schools and old-age homes, as well as homes for the homeless in the area. The childrens library is separate from the main library and has a great selection of books to cater for children of all ages. A separate study area is also available for students who do not want to be disturbed by the public. Group-loans are available for schools who wish to engage with the library. The schools and pre-schools nearby visit the library in groups to participate in Story hours and other holiday programmes offered here. Events are offered such as business workshops, public speaking workshops, youth programmes, Story Hour and Holiday Programmes. There are computeres available for public use.
