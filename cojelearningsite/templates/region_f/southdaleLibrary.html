Southdale Library

Southdale Library is inside Southdale shopping centre it is a very busy library servicing largely the Muslim community. Southdale library is committed to promote literature by making sure that everyone has equal access to library materials and services. The suburbs reached by the library are Southdale, Robertsham, Turffontein, Booysens, Ormonde, Crown Gardens, Chrisville and Bellavista. The library holds special programmes four times a year during school holidays children are encouraged to attend. We have Science Scuffle, Battle of the Books, Story Skirmish, Storytimes, visits from the Zoo, SPCA, fire department and a police detective teaching children about safety.
