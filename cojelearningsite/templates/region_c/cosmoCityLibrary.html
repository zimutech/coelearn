Cosmo City Library

Cosmo City Library is situated in Cosmo City Recreation Centre. Nearby by schools make it easirefor student to access the library. The collection at Cosmo City Library consists of adult and junior fiction and non-fiction books. There is a collection of reference and Stem books. We work closely with local schools and support educators. Programs that we host are Science Scuffle, Story Skirmish, Battle of the Books, Book clubs and Business workshops. The librarian also visits schools and pre-schools to read stories and provide training
