Boskruin Library

Boskruin Library is situated at Kelly Ave in Johannesburg. Our library is dedicated to constantly improving the community and perception of libraries by offering computer classes that enable both younger and older adults to improve, learn and stay engaged in a digital world. Our library has Internet enabled computers that the public can use for research and learning. The library environment is designed to be a safe space for kids when school are closed. We offer after school homework help, games and book clubs for all patrons. There are outreach programs designed to serve those community members that reside in remote areas and those who are homebound in order for them to also stay connected to the larger community.
