Hillbrow Library

Hillbrow Library is committed to fair equitable access to services, programmes and resources. The main aim of Hillbrow Library is to instill a culture of reading in its community members. Our library provides reading materials to different age groups, starting from toddler to pensioners. The users can take reading materials home, provided they got membership cards. Those without membership cards can use reading materials within the library building only. Every month we run programmes for all to ensure that other than providing books but we groom, train and educate our patrons.
