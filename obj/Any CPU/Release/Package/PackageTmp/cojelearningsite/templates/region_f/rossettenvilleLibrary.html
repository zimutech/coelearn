Rossettenville Library

Rossettenville Library is situated in Rossettenville shopping Centre. The Library caters for people from Rossettenville, Regents Park, La Rochelle, Turffontein and The Hill. The Library has a specialized African Display Corner that is aimed at promoting Pan-Africanism. Apart from the library collection, it also has programs aimed at early childhood development like Story Telling and other educational games. Youth Empowerment , promoting reading and writing are a major focus of the library. The library works closely with the nearby Rossettenville clinic, the local Primary Schools including Regentss Park Primary School. The local community and clinic are actively involved in empowerment programs and other activities that are aimed at community development.
