Blackheath Library

Blackheath Library is a busy but friendly community library situated in the heart of the Blackheath Heathway Centre. Our library is a community hub, where programmes are delivered, and people engage in lifelong learning. Blackheath Library ensures that community library programmes are in line with the 4th industrial revolution by offering information literacy and e-learning training programmes. The library also offers homework assistance to school children and also caters for them by creating a safe environment for learning. We have a good relationship with local schools and collaborate in programmes like Battle of the Book and Story Skirmish competitions with the aim of continuously developing and helping our community. The programmes are designed to promote literacy and stress the importance of libraries to juveniles and the community at large.
