Randburg Library

Our library is located in heart of Randburg and easly accessible to our local community members that we serve with passion and distinction. We are passionate about uplifting our Randburg local schools and are fully focused on community development, community outreach, upskilling and digital training. Our facilities are meant to be safe condusive environment for both studying and pleasureble reading. We host a variety incliding but not limited to story time, skirmish competitions, Business workshops and Battle of the Books to name a few. An educated reading nation is a nation that constently grows and prospers and we aim to facilite this in our establishment.
