Emmarentia Library

The library is situated within close proximity to the local Emmarentia schools which makes it convenient for learners to visit on a regular basis. Our aim as a library is to see constant improvement and continuous change in our community and a general adoption of a learning and prosporous local community. One of our highlights as a library is our adoption of the Pail computer program which is focused on empowering and training our local community. Since the introduction of this service, we have observed a difference in skills gained and growth in terms of library usage all with the aim of upskilling our patrons. The library has school holiday programmes that are designed for kids during school holidays to promote literacy, literature growth in the community. This program and others that we host are designed to keep our patrons engaged, entertained and constantly learning. We strive to create a good learning environment.
