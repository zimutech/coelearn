Parkhurst Library

The library is situated in the Recreational Facilities Center next to the Community Hall, Parkhurst Municipal Clinic and Verity Park in close proximity. The library ensures that they have an inviting and interesting space to read and study. There is a small study area in the library that provides a quiet place to study, and it has public internet access. During July and December school holidays, the library hosts picture frame cutting, face painting children programs, and story time. These are always well attended, and enjoyed by the children and their parents. The library works closely with local schools, nursery homes, businesses and enjoys the support of the Parkhurst primary school. Parkhurst day-care center in Parkhurst community brings the children every week to read books and for storytime.
