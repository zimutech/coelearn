Rosebank

The Rosebank Library extends their services beyond the physical walls of the building by providing study material and electronic means including connecting from home via overdrive. The library provides a study area which is quite and peaceful for users to utilize. The library host various programmes including Business Workshops and Youth workshops that are aimed at empowering community members by proving information and training on how to start a business, how to write CV's, applicatiocaton letters and how to use the internet to find jobs. There is a book club where members share exciting information about books and becoming a member is free.
