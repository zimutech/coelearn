Westbury Library

Westbury Library is committed to keeping pace with modern technology and the changing role of libraries. We have e-learning classroom where there are computers for the public to use. The library provides computer training for free. We have a quiet place to study and for group discussion. The library host programs during school holidays and have weekly storytimes for the pre-schools in the area. We have two reading competitions namely Story Skirmish and Battle of the Books for primary schools.
