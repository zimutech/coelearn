Florida Library

Library is the heart and soul of the community. There are more formal opportunities such as joining a friend of the library group or becoming a library trustee. Florida library is the community hub. In addition to connecting people to information, libraries connect people to people. The library has a large collection for the blind. The library works hand in hand with the National Library for the Blind in Gramstad. We have tape machines and books on tapes for the blind public of Roodepoort. The machines and tapes are available for loans for the members who are unable to go to Florida and the branch librarian make means for them to get it.
