Wilro Park Library

We are a very small and busy community library about 264m2 situated in the Wilropark shopping centre. We offer lending services, homework assistance, career guidance information, holiday programmes and story hours. We also do reference enquiries. We participate in Story Skirmish, Battle of the Books, Science Scuffle and we also do Holiday programmes and story hours.
