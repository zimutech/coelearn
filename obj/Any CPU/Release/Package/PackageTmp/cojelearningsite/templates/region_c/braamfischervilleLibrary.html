Braamfischerville Library

Braamfischerville Library is situated at Multipurpose Centre. We work closely with local schools and we support our local educators. The library has many educational programmes for everyone from juveniles to the seasoned generation. Programs that we host are Science scuffle, Story skirmish, Battle of thebooks and a variety of Business workshops. Our librarian also visits the local schools and pre-schools to engage with learners and read stories every Wednesday.
