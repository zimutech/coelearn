Leratong Container Library

Leratong Library provides a quiet area for studying, as well as common areas for collaboration. We have computers for patrons to access the internet, use for their personal reasons like creating cv, sending email and to access our electronic books on overdrive. Most of the library users are students because we are close to universities and colleges. Patrons use the library to do research, group work, and study. The library caters to all patrons, we offer programs that are suitable for all our users. There is storytime twice a week, Mondays and Thursdays. Every month we visit people with disabilities and read them stories.
