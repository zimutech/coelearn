Horizon View Library

Horizonview Library aims to ensure that the library remains an integral part of the community. The Horizonview Library is inside the recreation center. It provides physical and digital access to the material. Library's collection includes printed materials, digital access through overdrive, and other physical resources such as DVDs. There is a newly renovated Study area in the library which provides a quiet place to study. The library hosts storytimes each month and holiday programs during the school holidays. These are always well attended, and enjoyed by the children and their parents. The library does participate in Story Skirmish and Battle of the Books competitions.
