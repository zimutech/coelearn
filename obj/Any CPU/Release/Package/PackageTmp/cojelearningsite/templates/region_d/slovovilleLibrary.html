Slovoville Library

The library offers people free access to a wealth of information and other information related services, whether online or in print. We work closely with local schools and collaborate with the relevant authorities and educators. The library has many educational programmes for everyone from juvenile to seasoned members of the community. Programmes that we host are Science Scuffle, Story Skirmish, Battle of the books, and a variety of business workshops. The librarians also visit local schools and pre-school to read stories and provide training. Our library also provides homework assistant, career guidance, and information literacy for all users wishing to partake or learn. E-books are accessible by registered members of the library on overdrive. Slovoville Library is committed to community development and continual community support because it believes that a well read and educated community is one that thrives and can defeat social ills.
