Lenasia Ext 1 Library

Lenasia Ext 1 Library is situated in Lenasia Ext 1 Civic Centre. The library was built in 1970's by the SMLC municipality and it was approximately 1200 m2. It was next to Lenasia post office and later moved to Civic Centre it is opposite the taxi rank. It was also extended now and divided into various sections such as children section, lending section and study areas for tertiary students.The Library host various programmes including, Youth Workshops and Business Workshops that are aimed to empower community members by providing information and training on how to start businesses.
