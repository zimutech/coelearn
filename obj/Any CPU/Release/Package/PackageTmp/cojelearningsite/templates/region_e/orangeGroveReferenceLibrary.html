Orange Grove Reference Library

Orange Groove Library is staffed by two Librarians and a General worker. The library has a collection of reference books for adults, young adults and children's (fiction and non-fiction books). We provide a quiet place to study and have public internet access for free. Library provides homework assistance for children and picture colouring for pre-school kiddies if accompanied by gaurdian. Community newspaper is delivered every Tuesday with updated information for the area and surrounding surburbs. The library has a good collection of non-fictional and fictional books. Children and toddlers are catered for with a variety of books for beginners as we prioritise early childhood development. Our library hosts Story Time on Wednesday afternoons at 15:00. During public holidays, we host special holiday programmes in order to engage with the community.
