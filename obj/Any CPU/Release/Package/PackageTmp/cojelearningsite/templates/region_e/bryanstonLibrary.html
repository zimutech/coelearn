Bryanston Library

The library is staffed by two Librarians, two Senior Library Assistants and one General worker. The collection at Bryanston Library consists of adult fiction and non-fiction, young adult fiction and non-fiction, junior fiction and non-fiction plus a large print of books. There is a collection of reference for both adult and children. The audio-visual collection consists of CD's for adult and junior books read onto discs. This is a new service available at Bryanston Library: Tape aids for Blind are sponsoring 20 adult and 20 children audio books on CD in MP3 format. The will swop these books every 2 months. During March, July and December school holidays the library hosts holiday programmes. Every Wednesday at 2:30 pm Bryanston Library host chilidren story time.The Friends of the Bryston Library (FOBL) was formed in March 1998 to raise money to buy books for the Bryanston Library. Juliet Manning is the chairperson of Friends of Bryanston Library. FOBL members donate an annual amount choosing, and together with income derived from monthly FOBL book sales, they buy the latest books for the exclusive use of FOBL members.
