Alexandra Children's Library

The Alexandra 3rd Avenue Library is staffed with a Librarian, Library Assistant and a Care Taker. The library consists of junior fiction and non-fiction books. The monthly magazines of parliament of the Republic of South Africa and Alex newspapers are available in the library. We proudly have a newly renovated study centre in our library. We have reference books and picture books for the children in the library. We also have ten Pail computer to use and also provide free access to Wi-Fi in our Library. Monday to Friday different Creche's around Alex come to the library for story telling. We also assist kids with home work, career guidance and we also have reading programmes e.g Science Scuffle and Story Skirmish.
