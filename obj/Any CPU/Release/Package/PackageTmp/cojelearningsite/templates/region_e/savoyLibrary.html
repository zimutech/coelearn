Savoy Library

The Savoy Library is staffed by a Librarian and two Library assistats. The collection at Savoy Library consists of adult and junior fiction and non-fiction books. Magazines are available for swapping and for reading in the library and there is also a collection of reference books. These items are provided by the City of Johannesburg Municipality. There is no formal study area but tables and chairs are available in the main study area. Wi-Fi is available for free in the Library and study centre. During July and December school holidays the Librarynhosts story times. These are always well attended and enjoyed by the children. There is also a weekly story time. Savoy is part of the citywide ECD programme. Books are distributed to specific creches in the area to assist with teaching.
